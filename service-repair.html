Common Signs

Is Your AC Showing Any of These Signs?

Florida heat doesn't wait. If your AC is acting up, don't let it become a full breakdown. Our techs diagnose and fix all makes and models — same day.

📞 Call (321) 384-7868
⚡ AC blowing warm or hot air
⚡ Unit not turning on or short cycling
⚡ Weak or no airflow from vents
⚡ Strange noises — banging, rattling, squealing
⚡ Refrigerant or water leaking
⚡ Frozen evaporator coils
⚡ Thermostat not responding correctly
⚡ Drain line clogged or backing up
⚡ Tripping breakers or electrical issues
⚡ Uneven cooling between rooms
⚡ Bad or musty smell from vents
⚡ Electric bill suddenly spiking
