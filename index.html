Frequently Asked Questions


How quickly can you respond to an AC emergency?

We offer same-day service across Central Florida. For emergencies, our dispatchers are available 24/7 — often on-site within hours of your call.

How much does an AC tune-up cost?

Our standard AC tune-up is $89 flat rate with no hidden charges. Air Care+ members receive 2 annual tune-ups included in their $29/month membership — a savings of $198/year.

Do you offer financing for AC replacement?

Yes. We offer 0% financing options to make AC replacement accessible for every budget. Ask about available plans during your free quote.

What is the Air Care+ membership?

Air Care+ is our $29/month plan that includes 2 annual tune-ups ($198 value), 15% off all repairs and parts, priority emergency dispatch, no contracts, and your first month is completely free.

Are your technicians licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Soligo Air holds Florida HVAC license #CAC1823002. Every technician is EPA-certified, manufacturer-trained, and fully insured.
